Salmon Cookies

a seattle delicacy

What started out as a cruel joke on our grandmother who was a well known baker in the Seattle Area eventually became a point of pride for our family and the city of Seattle. We are Salmon Cookies.

We are a family owned and operated local business and proud member of the greater NorthWest community. All our product is wild and sustainably line-caught salmon. We are currently featuring our Chinook Salmon Cookies and will be featuring them at our kiosk in the upcoming Ballard SeafoodFest!

Our kiosk will have a cookie making workshop for the kids, merchandise, and arts an arts and crafts station featuring salmon-based paints that will have your art smelling like a real-life fishing vessel for years to come!
